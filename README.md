# Power BI Sales-Dashboard
This Sales Dashboard is an interactive data visualization solution designed to provide clear, actionable insights into sales performance across multiple dimensions such as time, geography, customer behavior, and product categories.

# The goal of this dashboard is to help stakeholders:

Monitor overall business performance
Identify profit trends and seasonal patterns
Analyze customer purchasing behavior
Evaluate product and category performance
Support data-driven decision-making

📈 Dashboard Features & Visualizations
# 1. Profit by Month
Displays monthly profit trends across the year.
Helps identify seasonality, peak sales periods, and loss-making months.
Positive and negative profits are clearly distinguished for better insight.
# 2. Sales Distribution by State
A horizontal bar chart showing total sales amount by state.
Highlights top-performing regions (e.g., Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh).
Useful for regional strategy and expansion planning.
# 3. Quantity by Payment Mode
A donut chart illustrating how customers prefer to pay.
Payment modes include:
Cash on Delivery (COD)
UPI
Debit Card
Credit Card
EMI
Helps understand customer payment behavior and optimize payment options.
# 4. Customer-wise Sales Contribution
Bar chart showing sales amount by individual customers.
Identifies high-value customers (e.g., Harivansh, Madhav).
Useful for customer segmentation and loyalty strategies.
# 5. Quantity by Product Category
Donut chart displaying distribution across categories:
Clothing
Electronics
Furniture
Highlights dominant categories (Clothing contributes the most).
Helps in inventory planning and category-level decision-making.
# 6. Profit by Sub-Category
Horizontal bar chart showing profit contribution by sub-categories:
Printers
Bookcases
Sarees
Accessories
Tables
Enables granular analysis of product profitability.
🎛️ Filters & Interactivity
Quarter Filter (Q1–Q4): Allows users to analyze performance for specific quarters.
State Filter: Enables region-specific insights.
Interactive visuals allow users to drill down and explore data dynamically.
